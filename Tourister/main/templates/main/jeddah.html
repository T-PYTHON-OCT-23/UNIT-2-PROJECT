Jeddah City

jeddah City Explore the Undiscovered:

The city of Jeddah, the bride of the Red Sea, and the largest city overlooking it, is distinguished by its geographical location as it is the gateway to the holiest places in the world. It is the first stop at which pilgrims arriving from all over the world land on their way to Mecca, through its international airport and sea port. It is also distinguished by Jeddah with its beautiful beaches.

King Abdullah Economic City

King Abdullah Economic City is a new Saudi city with an economic nature. Its establishment was announced by the Custodian of the Two Holy Mosques, King Abdullah bin Abdulaziz, in 2005 AD. It is located in Rabigh Governorate, the Makkah Al-Mukarramah region, in Al-Qudaymah and Sabar.
