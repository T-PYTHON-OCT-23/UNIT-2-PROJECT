Fayd City

Fayd is a historical city located to the southeast of the Hail region. It is considered one of the historical archaeological sites of the region, as its mountains store historical inscriptions dating back to prehistoric times.

Aaref Castle

Aarif Castle is a historical castle located in the heart of Hail city, Saudi Arabia. It sits atop a hill overlooking the city, which is how it got its name. The castle is believed to have been built in 1840 and is considered one of Hail's most important cultural landmarks. It is easily visible from anywhere in the city.
