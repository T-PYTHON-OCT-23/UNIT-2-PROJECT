Some Of the tourist Places In Abha

Al Habala Cable Car Station

This cable car route spans 600 meters on its way to the ancient Habala village, built by the Qahtan tribe nearly 400 years ago.

The High City

High City has an ideal strategic location at the heart of Abha since it is surrounded by many elegant hotels and captivating parks as well as it is easily reachable via King Abdulaziz Road from all parts of the city.

Rijal Almaa village in Aseer

A trip to Aseer is not complete without a visit to Rijal Almaa historical village, which is only 45 km away from Abha. With its stone-built castles and colourful buildings, it made it to the list of tourism villages of the world

Historical Tabab

Historical Tabab has historic and political importance, as it was the capital of Aseer region during the era of the First Saudi State. It is full of ancient castles, palaces and mosques dating back hundreds of years

Waterfall Park

One of the coziest parks in Abha, It is a famous tourist destination during the months of summer when the beautiful flowers around the park blooms. Waterfall Park is known for holding a wide variety of different plants and flowers

Abu Kheyal Park

Entering Abu Kheyal Park, located on King Abdulaziz Road, feels like walking through vast green fields that seem to extend beyond the horizon
